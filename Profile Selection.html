Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about registration and platform access

Can I change my user category after registration?

Yes, you can update your profile category at any time through your account settings. However, some features may require additional verification when switching categories.

What documents do I need for registration?

Requirements vary by category. Civil servants need staff ID, while SMEs need business registration. All users need valid identification and contact information.

Is there a registration fee?

No, registration on the Zamfara Digital Platform is completely free for all users as part of the state's digital inclusion initiative.

How long does verification take?

Most registrations are verified within 24-48 hours. Civil servant verification may take up to 72 hours as it involves cross-checking with government databases.

Can I access the platform offline?

Yes! The platform works offline. You can download content when online and access it later without an internet connection. Your progress will sync when you're back online.

Is the platform available in Hausa?

Yes! You can switch between English and Hausa using the language toggle at the top right of the page. More languages will be added soon.
